About Pushp Niketan

Our story, vision, mission, philosophy, and the people who make PNS exceptional.

🌳
Est. 2005
Two decades of excellence in progressive education
🌿 Our Story

Blooming for 20 Years

Pushp Niketan School is located in the semi-urban town of Dhampur, in the Dhampur Sugar Mills campus, Bijnor district, Uttar Pradesh. It sits in a beautiful, lush green campus — a place loved by children and families alike.

The school was envisioned to be a world-class model for progressive education with child-centric philosophy, pedagogies, and processes. We established a non-hierarchical, alternative school promoting excellence in learning and sound values in a safe, supportive, and stimulating environment.

As a non-profit venture, we serve students from Dhampur and the surrounding rural communities — bringing high-quality, inquiry-based education to those who need it most. Our inspiration: "A lot is happening in cities — let's raise the quality of life and education in small towns like Dhampur."

🎯 Vision & Mission

What Drives Everything We Do

🌱 Our Vision

To be an exemplary institution where sound ethical values, character, wisdom, leadership, service, and academic achievement are encouraged — in a safe and nurturing environment — honing skills and preparing students to be global citizens.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide world-class, child-centric education that nurtures the whole child — intellectually, emotionally, physically, and morally — using innovative, inquiry-based pedagogies in a non-hierarchical, inclusive environment.

❤️ Our Values

Integrity, curiosity, empathy, creativity, and community service. We believe every child is unique and has a special contribution to make — our role is to discover and nurture that gift.

💡 Our Approach

Multiple intelligences, thematic and inquiry-based learning, experiential education, and teacher autonomy combine to create a dynamic, joyful learning environment where both students and teachers grow together.

🧠 Our Philosophy

The PNS Way of Learning

Built on decades of research and passion, our educational philosophy sets us apart from every conventional school.

🧒

Child-Centric

Every decision we make starts with the child's needs, curiosity, and unique learning style. Children are active agents in their own education.

🔄

Non-Hierarchical

Teachers learn from students. Students teach each other. Our flat structure encourages dialogue, respect, and collaborative growth at every level.

🔬

Inquiry-Based

We ask "why?" more than we give answers. Thematic, project-based learning builds critical thinking, problem-solving, and a love of discovery.

🌍

Multiple Intelligences

We recognize that children are smart in many different ways — linguistic, mathematical, musical, kinesthetic, artistic, interpersonal, and more.

🤝

Community Focused

We serve Dhampur and its rural surroundings, believing that world-class education should not be limited to big cities — it belongs here too.

✨

Holistic Growth

Academic excellence, emotional well-being, physical health, and moral character — we develop the complete human being, not just the student.

👩‍💼 Principal's Message

A Message from Our Principal

Ms. Rashmi Rekha leads Pushp Niketan School with a vision of creating a joyful, inclusive, and academically excellent school community. Under her leadership, the school has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the Bijnor district.

She expertly guides the school's orientation programmes, curriculum development, and teacher training — always keeping the child at the centre of every initiative. Her approach — warm, structured, and forward-thinking — has shaped the culture and values of Pushp Niketan School.

Her shared goal, expressed at every orientation: "Nurturing children to reach their full potential."
